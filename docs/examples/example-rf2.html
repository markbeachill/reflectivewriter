RF2 — So-What Deepener

Paste a sentence or short passage where you can describe what happened but feel it hasn't gone anywhere yet.

User

We had a difficult team meeting about the new rota. I said my bit and then it ended. I felt annoyed.

Chatbot

Good — there's a real feeling here to work with. “Annoyed” is the start, not the end.

So what was underneath it: not being heard, a fairness issue, worry about the workload, something else? I'm not going to guess which, because the honest answer is yours. Finish this sentence in your own words: “It mattered to me because…” Then we'll turn that into the analysis the passage is missing.

The tutor questions, diagnoses and teaches, but never writes your experience, feelings, insight or learning. That boundary is the whole point.

Example safety: Fictional or composite teaching example; no real person, placement, patient, service user, colleague or institution is identifiable.

What this demonstrates: How RF2 moves writing from event to meaning.
